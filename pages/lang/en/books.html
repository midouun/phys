Recommended Books

Here are some books I recommend for further reading in Physics and Computer Science.

Physics Books

Physics for Scientists and Engineers

By: Raymond A. Serway, John W. Jewett

A comprehensive textbook covering classical and modern physics.

Fundamentals of Physics

By: David Halliday, Robert Resnick, Jearl Walker

An excellent resource for introductory physics courses.

Computer Science Books

Introduction to Algorithms

By: Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein

A classic textbook on algorithms and data structures.

Clean Code

By: Robert C. Martin

Essential reading for software developers on writing clean, maintainable code.
