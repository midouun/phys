About Samahi Abdelhak

Samahi Abdelhak

Master's Degree: Inorganic Chemistry

Language Level: A1 in English, A1 in French

Graduate of: University of Saida

Teaching Experience: 3 years

Location: Aïn El Hadjar, Saïda, Algeria

Welcome to my professional profile. I am Samahi Abdelhak, a dedicated physics teacher with a passion for making complex scientific concepts accessible and engaging for students. My academic background in Inorganic Chemistry provides a strong foundation for understanding the fundamental principles that underpin physics.

I believe in fostering a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, question, and discover. My goal is to inspire a love for science and critical thinking, preparing students not just for exams, but for a lifetime of curiosity and learning.
