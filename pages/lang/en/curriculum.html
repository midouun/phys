Full-Year Physics Teaching Plan

Chapter Description Goals Lessons
Chapter 1: Introduction to Physics Basic concepts, scientific method, units and measurements. Understand fundamental principles and scientific inquiry. View Lessons
Chapter 2: Kinematics Motion in one and two dimensions, displacement, velocity, acceleration. Analyze and describe motion using mathematical models. View Lessons
Chapter 3: Dynamics Newton's Laws of Motion, forces, friction, circular motion. Apply Newton's laws to solve problems involving forces and motion. View Lessons
